WHY NAME CHANGE? BETTER FOR EVERYONE

As part of our re-branding plan, we are now USTechnology - Avant Latin America. Together with our engineering and manufacturing in Texas we now enjoy an increased memory production, reduced lead times, superior quality control and improved memory availability. As USTechnology - Avant Latin America, we will continue to provide our customers with top-quality memory, wholesale distribution options and professional account management.
